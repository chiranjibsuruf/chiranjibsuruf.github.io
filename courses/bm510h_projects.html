Project

A project shows you can analyze a real organizational problem and design an information system solution with clear workflows, data needs, and measurable benefits. It becomes a strong portfolio piece because it demonstrates business + tech thinking, stakeholder focus, and implementation readiness.

List of Probable Project Topics

  • MIS for Student/Academic Operations (admissions, attendance, grades, analytics dashboards)
  • Hospital/Clinic MIS (appointments, pharmacy, billing, patient history, KPI reporting)
  • HR MIS (recruitment workflow, onboarding, leave, performance dashboards)
  • Inventory & Procurement MIS (stock tracking, reorder triggers, supplier performance)
  • Sales & CRM MIS (lead pipeline, customer segmentation, sales forecasting reports)
  • Helpdesk / IT Service Management MIS (ticketing, SLA tracking, escalation analytics)
  • Project Management MIS (resource allocation, timelines, risk register, status reporting)
  • Finance MIS (budgeting, expense approvals, variance analysis, reporting)
  • Supply Chain Visibility MIS (order tracking, delivery SLAs, bottleneck dashboards)
  • Quality Management MIS (defect logging, corrective actions, audit trails, metrics)
  • E-commerce Operations MIS (orders, returns, customer support, fulfillment KPIs)
  • Library/Resource MIS (catalog, usage analytics, acquisition planning)
  • Smart Campus MIS (facility booking, maintenance tickets, energy monitoring)
  • NGO/CSR Program MIS (beneficiary tracking, outcomes, donor reports)
  • Manufacturing Shop-Floor MIS (production logs, downtime, OEE dashboards)
  • Transportation/Logistics MIS (fleet tracking, route efficiency, cost dashboards)
  • Compliance & Audit MIS (policy tracking, approvals, documentation, alerts)
  • Executive Decision Support System (DSS) (integrated KPIs + scenario analysis)
